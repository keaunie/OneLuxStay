About One Lux Stay

One Lux Stay curates refined, design-driven suites in the world’s most inspiring cities bringing together the privacy of a residence with the polish of a luxury hotel.

A New Language of Luxury Living

One Lux Stay was born from a simple idea: that every journey deserves a home that feels considered, calm, and beautifully put together. Our suites are designed as elevated sanctuaries spaces where business travelers, couples, and long-term guests can unwind, recharge, and truly live.

From Antwerp’s historic Diamond District to the oceanfront energy of Miami and the skyline views of Dubai and Los Angeles, each residence is hand-selected for its location and thoughtfully styled with modern furnishings, warm lighting, and hotel-grade comforts.

Every detail—soft textiles, curated art, reliable high-speed Wi-Fi, and seamless check-ins is intentionally chosen to create a serene, effortless experience from the moment you arrive.

Our Mission

Our mission is to redefine short-term luxury living by pairing beautifully crafted interiors with intuitive, tech-enabled hospitality. We believe that feeling “at home” should never mean compromising on elegance, comfort, or convenience.

Whether for a weekend, a work trip, or an extended stay, we aim to offer the same standard across every One Lux Stay property: thoughtfully designed spaces, prime locations, and a consistently elevated guest experience.
