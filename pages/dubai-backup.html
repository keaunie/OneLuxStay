POPULAR ATTRACTIONS IN DUBAI

Live in the Heart of Dubai's Most Iconic Neighborhood


Burj Khalifa

The world's tallest building at 828 meters, offering breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks. It’s a must-see for anyone visiting Dubai.

Dubai Mall

The largest shopping and entertainment complex in the world, featuring over 1,200 shops, an ice rink, an aquarium, and an indoor theme park.

Dubai Fountain

Located outside Dubai Mall, this spectacular water and light show features jets of water dancing to music, set against the backdrop of the Burj Khalifa.

Dubai Opera

A stunning architectural masterpiece hosting performances ranging from opera and ballet to concerts and theater, all set in the heart of Downtown Dubai.

At The Top, Burj Khalifa

A visit to the observation decks on the 124th and 148th floors of Burj Khalifa offers unmatched views of Dubai and beyond, including a stunning sunset experience.

The Dubai Frame

A unique structure offering panoramic views of the city's old and new districts. It’s one of Dubai’s most distinctive landmarks, combining history with modernity.

Dubai City Walk

A vibrant urban development with a mix of shopping, dining, and entertainment options, offering a contemporary European-style ambiance with Dubai's signature luxury.

Souk Al Bahar

A traditional Arabic market just across from the Dubai Mall, featuring boutiques, galleries, and restaurants. It offers a unique blend of traditional and modern design.

Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo

Inside Dubai Mall, this massive aquarium houses thousands of aquatic animals. It also features a 270-degree tunnel for an immersive experience.

Jumeirah Beach Road & La Mer

A short drive from Downtown, La Mer is a trendy beachfront district with restaurants, shops, and art installations, perfect for a leisurely day out.

The Dubai Mall Ice Rink

An Olympic-sized ice rink in the heart of Dubai Mall, offering a cool escape from the heat with public skating sessions and special events.

Dubai Design District (d3)

A hub for creative minds, this area is home to art galleries, design studios, and trendy cafes, showcasing Dubai's growing influence in the global design world.

Dubai Transportation Options

One Lux Stay apartments are perfectly located near major transportation hubs, providing convenient access for getting around Dubai for every traveler looking to explore the city and beyond.


Dubai Trams

Serves areas like Dubai Marina and Jumeirah Beach Residence (JBR), providing a smooth and scenic travel option for shorter distances.

Dubai Metro

A fast, efficient rail system connecting Downtown Dubai, Dubai Marina, and the airport—perfect for budget-friendly city travel.

Taxis in Dubai

Widely available, metered, and affordable, Dubai's taxis are a convenient option for door-to-door travel. Hail one on the street or book through the Careem app.

RTA Buses

A comprehensive bus network covering the entire city, including major attractions. Air-conditioned and economical, it’s a great option for those on a budget.

Ride-Hailing Apps

Apps like Uber and Careem offer private car rides with flexible pick-up and drop-off options, providing a more personalized experience than taxis.

Dubai Ferry

Take a scenic ride along Dubai's coastline, including Dubai Marina and Dubai Creek, offering stunning views of landmarks like Burj Khalifa and Palm Jumeirah.

Abra (Traditional Boats)

Cross Dubai Creek on these traditional wooden boats, offering an authentic and inexpensive way to experience the city’s heritage.

Rental Cars

Rent a car for the ultimate flexibility in exploring Dubai and nearby emirates. The roads are well-maintained, and parking is widely available.

E-Scooters and Bikes

Available in select areas like Downtown Dubai, these are eco-friendly, convenient, and fun ways to explore nearby attractions.

Airport Transfers

Many hotels and tour companies offer private or shared transfers from Dubai International Airport, ensuring a seamless arrival experience.

Palm Jumeirah Monorail

The Palm Jumeirah Monorail offers scenic rides across the iconic Palm Jumeirah, connecting attractions like Atlantis, The Palm and Nakheel Mall.

Dubai Water Taxis

Operated by the RTA, they connect key locations like Dubai Marina, Dubai Creek, and Dubai Festival City, offering a unique view of Dubai’s skyline.
