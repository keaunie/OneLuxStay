Popular Attractions Around Hollywood

Our Hollywood and nearby apartments keep you close to LA’s most iconic cultural, coastal, and entertainment hotspots.

Hollywood Walk of Fame

A world-famous sidewalk honoring celebrities with over 2,600 brass stars embedded along Hollywood Boulevard.

Griffith Observatory

An iconic observatory with stunning views of Los Angeles and a public telescope for stargazing.

Universal Studios Hollywood

A thrilling movie-themed amusement park with rides, shows, and the famous Studio Tour.

Santa Monica Pier

A historic pier featuring an amusement park, aquarium, and family-friendly attractions along the beach.

LA County Museum of Art

The largest art museum in the western United States with extensive collections across multiple genres.

The Broad Museum

A contemporary art museum with a vast collection of postwar and contemporary art.

Venice Beach

A lively beach known for its iconic boardwalk, Muscle Beach gym, vibrant street art, and upbeat atmosphere.

Rodeo Drive Beverly Hills

A world-renowned shopping street in Beverly Hills known for luxury boutiques and designer stores.

The Getty Center

A renowned art museum with impressive collections and gardens offering panoramic views of Los Angeles.

NHMLA

A fascinating museum with exhibits on dinosaurs, mammals, and the natural world.

La Brea Tar Pits and Museum

An active paleontological site with fossils from the Ice Age and fascinating excavation exhibits.

Exposition Park

A cultural hub featuring museums, lush gardens, the LA Memorial Coliseum, and vibrant public spaces.

Getting Around Hollywood & Greater LA

Our Hollywood residences are close to major transport links, making it easy to explore the rest of Los Angeles and beyond.

Metro Rail System

Red and B Lines connect Hollywood with Downtown LA, Universal City, and other key destinations.

Metro Bus System

A comprehensive bus network with over 200 routes serving the entire LA County.

FlyAway Bus

Direct, non-stop bus service between LAX and popular destinations in LA.

Metrolink

A commuter train service connecting LA with neighboring counties.

Dodger Stadium Express

Free bus service to Dodger Stadium for ticket holders.

Uber & Lyft

On-demand rideshare services available throughout LA.

Bird & Lime Scooters

Electric scooters for short trips around LA neighborhoods.

Big Blue Bus

A public bus service primarily operating in Santa Monica and West LA.

LAX Airport Shuttles

Shared or private shuttle services to and from Los Angeles International Airport.

LA Yellow Cab

Traditional taxi service operating throughout Los Angeles.

Amtrak Pacific Surfliner

Scenic train service connecting Los Angeles with coastal cities in California.

Beverly Hills Trolley Tour

A charming trolley tour showcasing Beverly Hills landmarks.
