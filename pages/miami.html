Popular Attractions in Miami

Live in the heart of Miami’s most iconic neighborhoods and experiences.

Brickell City Centre

Luxury shopping, fine dining, and an open-air design just steps from Brickell’s towers and residences.

Mary Brickell Village

A lively hub for restaurants, bars, and boutiques — perfect for evenings out or casual strolls.

Miami Riverwalk

Scenic pathway along the Miami River with waterfront views, outdoor dining, and access to boat tours.

The Underline

Urban park and trail beneath the Metrorail featuring art, green spaces, and fitness areas.

Bayfront Park

Waterfront park with open lawns, an amphitheater, and sweeping views of the Miami skyline.

Jungle Island

Family-friendly attraction with exotic animals, interactive exhibits, and lush gardens.

Pérez Art Museum Miami (PAMM)

Bayfront museum for international modern and contemporary art with striking architecture.

Miami Circle National Historic Landmark

Archaeological site dating back 2,000 years, offering a glimpse into Miami’s ancient past.

Phillip and Patricia Frost Museum of Science

Interactive science museum featuring a planetarium, aquarium, and hands-on exhibits.

Brickell Key Park

Peaceful island park with bay views — ideal for jogging, walking, or relaxing by the water.

Kaseya Center

Home of the Miami Heat and a major venue for concerts and large entertainment events.

Miami Seaquarium & Virginia Key

Marine exhibits and island views, perfect to pair with a scenic coastal drive or paddle.

Miami Transportation Options

Stay near key transit hubs — explore Miami, Miami Beach, and the greater South Florida region with ease.

Metrobus (Miami-Dade Transit)

Wide bus network connecting neighborhoods, beaches, and major attractions with A/C coaches and frequent service.

Metrorail

Fast elevated rail linking Downtown/Brickell, Coconut Grove, Coral Gables, Dadeland and the Airport via the Orange Line.

Metromover (Free)

Free driverless loops around Downtown & Brickell — ideal for quick hops between museums, parks, and offices.

Brightline

Premium intercity rail from MiamiCentral to Fort Lauderdale, Boca Raton, West Palm Beach, and Orlando.

Tri-Rail (Commuter)

Budget-friendly commuter rail connecting Miami Airport Station with Broward and Palm Beach counties.

Miami Trolley (Free)

City-run, free trolley lines serving Brickell, Downtown, Wynwood, Little Havana and more.

Water Taxi & Bay Ferries

Scenic boat routes on Biscayne Bay connecting waterfront hotspots with skyline views.

Bike Share & E-Scooters

Citi Bike Miami and select e-scooter zones make short trips around the beach and downtown easy and eco-friendly.

Uber & Lyft

On-demand rideshare services that quickly connect you to any destination in Miami.

Miami International Airport (MIA)

Use the free MIA Mover to reach the Intermodal Center for Metrorail, Tri-Rail, buses, and the Rental Car Center.

PortMiami & Cruise Transfers

Shuttles, taxis, rideshare and private transfers link Brickell, MIA, and the cruise terminals.

Rental Cars

Pick up at MIA’s Rental Car Center — perfect for exploring Key Biscayne, Coral Gables, or day-trips to the Keys.
