POPULAR ATTRACTIONS IN REDONDO BEACH

Our apartments are ideally situated near Redondos’ top attractions, including iconic sites that showcase everything from Redondo Beach Pier to cultural landmarks.


Redondo Beach Pier (Municipal Pier)

Redondo Beach’s most iconic landmark! A lively, horseshoe-shaped pier buzzing with ocean-view dining, boutique shops, and endless coastal charm. Walk above the rolling waves, cast a fishing line, or catch a breathtaking sunset you’ll never forget.

Hopkins Wilderness Park

A lush retreat with forest trails, native plants, and peaceful picnic spots across four unique ecological zones. Escape into a wooded haven of trails, meadows, and native beauty — a tranquil contrast to Redondo’s lively coast.

South Bay Bike Path (The Strand) "Marvin Braude Bike Trail"

Ride along The Strand, where the coastline stretches onward in endless view. This stunning 22-mile coastal trail flows from Will Rogers State Beach through Redondo and ends at Torrance Beach. Cruise past piers and surf spots, dip into beach cities, and soak in the rhythm of sun, sea, and breeze.

Riviera Village

A charming, walkable district in Redondo filled with boutiques, art galleries, cozy cafés, and restaurants. It’s a local favorite for strolling, shopping, and sampling a variety of cuisines all within a relaxed village setting.

South Bay Galleria

Shop, dine, and unwind at South Bay Galleria, a vibrant three-story mall featuring over 100 stores, a modern movie theater, and diverse dining options. From fashion and tech to family entertainment, it’s the perfect indoor escape just minutes from the coast.

Redondo Beach Historical Museum

Discover Redondo Beach’s past at this quaint museum housed in a beautifully preserved 1904 home. Explore exhibits of early seaside life, local legends, and maritime history that shaped the city’s coastal identity.

The Bull Pen

A Redondo Beach legend since 1948, The Bull Pen blends rich history with bold flavors - from juicy steaks to signature cocktails and live evening tunes. It’s the kind of place where locals linger, laughter flows, and every meal feels like a celebration.

Bluewater Grill

Dine seaside at Bluewater Grill, where fresh, sustainable seafood pairs with harbor views, craft cocktails, and a relaxed coastal vibe, perfect for any occasion.

King Harbor Marina

Anchored in the heart of Redondo Beach, King Harbor Marina blends scenic ocean views with vibrant waterfront energy. Watch sailboats drift by, enjoy seaside dining and shops along the marina, or rent a kayak and explore the calm, sparkling harbor waters.

Salt & Pearl

Nestled in Riviera Village, Salt & Pearl serves an elevated mix of seafood and steaks in a stylish yet relaxed setting. Think fresh ocean fare, craft cocktails, and warm hospitality.

Knott's Berry Farm

a 57-acre amusement park in Buena Park, California, United States, owned and operated by Six Flags. In March 2015, it was ranked as the twelfth-most-visited theme park in North America, while averaging approximately 4 million visitors per year.

Manhattan Beach Pier

a pier located in Manhattan Beach, California, on the coast of the Pacific Ocean.

Redondo Transportation Options

One Lux Stay apartments are perfectly located near major transportation hubs, providing convenient access for getting around Redondo for every traveler looking to explore the city and beyond.


Fixed-Route Bus (Beach Cities Transit)

Local bus lines (e.g., Beach Cities Transit 102 & 109) link Redondo Beach with nearby South Bay communities.

Shared Ride Dial-A-Ride (Senior / Disabled Service)

Door-to-door ride service for seniors and persons with disabilities.

Bike Share / Bike Rentals

Pay-as-you-go bikes and e-bikes available for short coastal rides and sightseeing.

Ride-Hailing / Taxi

On-demand car services like Uber, Lyft, and local taxis for flexible point-to-point travel.

Car Rental

Rent a car from nearby providers for day trips and city exploration.

Electric Scooter / Micro-Mobility

Lightweight electric scooters perfect for quick coastal commutes.

Personal Cycling / Bike Path

Scenic bike paths stretch along the shoreline for easy coastal rides.

Park & Ride / Carpool

Park your car and share rides or connect with public transit downtown.

Electric Vehicle (EV) Travel / Charging

EV drivers enjoy multiple public charging points throughout the South Bay area.

Walking / Pedestrian Routes

A walkable seaside city with pedestrian-friendly boulevards and oceanfront paths.

Airport / Hotel Shuttle

Shared shuttles and vans connect Redondo Beach hotels with LAX and major transit centers.

Private Charter / Limousine Service
